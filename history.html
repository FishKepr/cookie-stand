Our Story

A flash of Inspiration

Our company was founded by a stay-at-home looking for a way to get those important omega-3 nutrients inter her family diet. As she watched her ever moving children grab snacks off the kitchen counter and go, the idea for a treat suddenly hit her: "Could I really do this?" She asked herself. Her answer came in the form of a movie.

"Why Not?"

While watching the film "We Bought a Zoo", the tag line struck her. "Why not?" So she gave it a try and Salmon Cookies were born.

Her Greatest fans (and Critics)

As she perfected the recipies, her family performed the taste testing. Naturally, she created a few failures (to put it politely), but they never stopped encouraging her to keep trying. Eventually she hit on the right combination and unveiled Salmon Cookies to the world.
